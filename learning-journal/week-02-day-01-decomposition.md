2. verify there is at least one tea bag in the box
3. get a kettle
4. verify the kettle can hold water without leaking
5. do you want to use a gas stove? if yes, verify that there is enough gas left to boil water.
6. Or do you want to use the electric cooker? if yes, verify the cooker is connected to a working electric power source.
7. rinse the kettle
8. take a tea bag from the box and put it in the kettle
9. light the gas stove or plug in the electric cooker to the power source.
10. put the kettle on the gas stove or electric cooker.
11. heat the water in the kettle until it starts boiling.
12. Let it boil for about 5 more minutes
13. put the gas stove off  or disconnect the electric cooker from the source
14. Wash or rinse the tea jug
15. pour the tea in the kettle into the jug
16. Wash or rinse the teacup
17. pour some of the tea into the cup

Decomposition 4 (ClinicLink Appointment)
1. A patient log in to his ClinicLink account
2. Click on "Book Appointment" button
3. Patient select Date and time
4. The system checks if the selected date and time has not been chosen by another patient already
5. if yes, the system prompts the patient to choose another time and date (suggesting available date and time closest to the previously chosen time and date. The patient can specify another date and time outside the suggestion.)
6. The system checks again if the newly chosen time is free
7. if yes, the system sends an appointment confirmation message to the patient
8. The system sends appointment reminder message to the patient on a regular basis.
